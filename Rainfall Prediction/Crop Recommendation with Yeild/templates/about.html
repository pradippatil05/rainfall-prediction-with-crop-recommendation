This is what We do.

A Rainfall prediction is the process of forecasting the amount and timing of precipitation in a particular area. It is an important tool for farmers, water resource managers, and emergency responders, as well as for the general public. Accurate rainfall prediction can help farmers plan their planting and harvesting schedules, water resource managers allocate water resources more efficiently, and emergency responders prepare for potential floods or other weather-related disasters. In this system also provide crop prediction which is basis on amount of Rainfall and other parameters this is very useful for farmers also.

Crop recommendation system built by using Machine Learning. with a motive of helping farmers to choose a right crop for a. particular area of land. Soil and temperature are considered. In order to mitigate the agrarian crisis in the current status quo, there is a need for better recommendation systems to alleviate the crisis by helping the farmers to make an informed decision before starting the cultivation of cropsBased on soil, surface temperature and rainfall, crop recommendation system provides accurate crop selection.
